01 / Portraits

Individual portrait sessions

A relaxed, guided session built around natural light and your own character. Whether you are visiting Athens or based here, I create space for you to feel at ease and be yourself. Sessions take place across the city and the Riviera, at timing chosen for the best light.

1 or 2 hours 40 plus edited images Private gallery Film available

02 / Couples

Couples and engagement sessions

Candid, unscripted, and built around the two of you. Whether you are celebrating an anniversary, newly engaged, or simply in Athens together, I photograph what actually happens between people. No rigid posing. Just your story, told honestly.

1 or 2 hours 40 plus edited images 2 location changes included Film available

03 / Weddings

Destination weddings in Greece

I treat your wedding as a cinematic story, focused on natural light and the moments that cannot be staged. Based on the Athens Riviera and available across the Greek islands and mainland. Half day and full day collections, digital and 35mm film options. Travel outside Athens arranged separately.

Half day or full day 200 to 450 images Sneak peek within 72 hours Film in top collection

04 / Business

Business portraits and brand campaigns

Professional portraits for founders, teams and creatives, alongside brand campaign photography for launches, editorial and commercial use. I work to bring out the character behind the brand — clean, confident and genuinely you.

Individual or team Brand campaigns High resolution delivery Quick turnaround available

05 / Film

35mm analog film

Film is available as an add-on to any session or wedding collection. I shoot on 35mm, developed and scanned to high resolution. Each roll yields 10 to 15 scanned frames delivered alongside your digital gallery.

10 to 15 frames per roll Add-on to any session Delivered with digital gallery
